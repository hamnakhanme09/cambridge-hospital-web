Our Journey

A Legacy of Compassionate Care

Founded on a Promise of Health

**Cambridge Hospital** began its journey over four decades ago with a clear vision: to bring world-class, affordable healthcare to the heart of the community. What started as a modest clinic has blossomed into a premier academic medical center, renowned for its commitment to patient care, education, and research.

Our founders believed that healthcare should be a right, not a privilege. This principle still guides every decision, ensuring we provide comprehensive services to all individuals, irrespective of their socio-economic status.

Our Historical Milestones

The Humble Beginning

Cambridge Hospital is officially founded as a small, 50-bed community clinic dedicated to basic general health services and maternal care. This year laid the groundwork for our community-first philosophy.

1985

Major Expansion & Specialist Centers

The hospital completes its first major expansion, increasing capacity to 300 beds. We launch the Heart & Vascular Institute and the Cancer Care Centre, introducing advanced surgical and oncological services to the region.

1998

Academic & Research Affiliation

We establish a formal partnership with the Cambridge Medical University, solidifying our role as a major teaching hospital. This marks the beginning of our commitment to medical research and training the next generation of doctors.

2010

Adoption of Digital Health

Implementation of a comprehensive Electronic Health Records (EHR) system across all departments and the launch of our patient portal, making care more efficient, secure, and accessible for the community.

2018

Excellence and Global Recognition

Today, Cambridge Hospital serves over 1 million patients annually, maintains a 98% patient satisfaction rate, and is continually recognized for clinical excellence in multiple specialties. The journey continues!
