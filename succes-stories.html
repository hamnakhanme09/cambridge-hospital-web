Patient Triumphs

The Marathon Runner's Second Chance

Patient: **Mr. Omar Khan** | Department: **Heart & Vascular Institute** | Date: October 2025

Omar, an avid marathon runner, suddenly collapsed due to a rare congenital heart defect. Our rapid-response team performed a complex, life-saving surgery. *He was told he'd never run again, but his spirit and our team's expertise had other plans...*

The four-hour procedure required precision and innovative robotic techniques, which minimized his recovery time. After months of intensive cardiac rehabilitation with our physical therapists, Omar didn't just walk—he trained. Exactly one year after his surgery, he completed a half-marathon, crossing the finish line with the very surgeon who saved his life cheering him on. His success is a testament to the cutting-edge technology and compassionate continuity of care at Cambridge Hospital.

Healing Beyond the Cure: Sara’s Oncology Journey

Patient: **Ms. Sara Ali** | Department: **Cancer Care Centre** | Date: September 2025

Diagnosed with aggressive Stage III breast cancer, Sara felt overwhelmed. Her journey involved chemotherapy, radiation, and a delicate surgery. *More than the treatment, it was the support—the holistic care—that made all the difference in her darkest hours...*

Our Oncology Support Services, including dedicated nutritionists, social workers, and art therapy programs, provided Sara with strength. The precision of her targeted radiation therapy minimized side effects, allowing her to maintain a good quality of life during treatment. Today, Sara is in remission and volunteers at the hospital, giving hope to newly diagnosed patients. Her story embodies our belief that treating the person, not just the disease, is the true mark of excellent cancer care.
