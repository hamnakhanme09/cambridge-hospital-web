Our Comprehensive Programs

Undergraduate Medical Program (MBBS)

Our integrated curriculum blends basic sciences with clinical training from the very first year. The program emphasizes **problem-based learning (PBL)**, early patient contact, and ethical reasoning, preparing students to be competent and compassionate physicians.

  • **Pre-Clinical (Years 1-2):** Anatomy, Physiology, Biochemistry, Pharmacology, Pathology, Community Medicine.
  • **Clinical Rotations (Years 3-5):** Core specialties like Internal Medicine, General Surgery, Paediatrics, Obstetrics & Gynaecology, and Psychiatry.
  • **Duration:** 5 Years followed by 1 Year of Internship.

Postgraduate Residency & Fellowships

We offer highly competitive and globally recognized residency programs accredited by international bodies. Trainees gain exposure to a diverse patient population and complex cases across our specialized institutes.

**Residency:** Internal Medicine, Surgery, Radiology, etc.

**Fellowships:** Cardiology, Neuro-Surgery, Oncology.

Focus on research and teaching skills.

Innovation in Medical Research

Clinical Trials Unit

Dedicated to evidence-based medicine and pioneering research. We run Phase I-IV trials in Oncology, Cardiology, and Neurosciences, translating discovery into patient benefit.

Biomedical Informatics

Utilizing **AI and Big Data** to analyze patient outcomes, improve diagnostics, and personalize treatment plans. Our biobank supports genetic and molecular research.

Global Health Initiatives

Partnerships with international institutions to address global health disparities, focusing on infectious diseases and public health policy in underserved communities.
