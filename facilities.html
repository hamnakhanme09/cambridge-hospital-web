Our World-Class Facilities

At Cambridge Hospital, we are dedicated to providing state-of-the-art medical facilities and a comfortable environment for all our patients. From advanced diagnostic centers to modern patient rooms, every aspect of our hospital is designed with your health and well-being in mind. We continually invest in technology and infrastructure to ensure the highest standards of care.

Patient Rooms

General Wards
  • Spacious and well-ventilated
  • Round-the-clock nursing care
  • Economical options

Our general wards offer a comfortable and caring environment, ensuring patients receive constant medical attention in a shared space.

Private Rooms
  • Enhanced privacy and comfort
  • Attached washroom facilities
  • Air-conditioned

For patients requiring more privacy, our private rooms come with all essential amenities and a serene atmosphere for recovery.

Deluxe Suites
  • Premium comfort and luxury
  • Dedicated attendant area
  • Advanced entertainment options

Our deluxe suites offer a superior experience with added amenities and personalized services for utmost comfort during stay.

Intensive Care Units (ICU)

General ICU
  • Advanced Monitoring
  • Ventilator Support
  • 24/7 Specialist Care

Equipped with state-of-the-art technology, our General ICU provides critical care for patients with severe medical conditions.

Cardiac Intensive Care Unit (CICU)
  • Specialized Cardiac Monitoring
  • Expert Cardiologist Supervision
  • Advanced Life Support Systems

Dedicated to cardiac patients, our CICU offers specialized care and monitoring for heart-related critical conditions.

Surgical Intensive Care Unit (SICU)
  • Post-operative Critical Care
  • Infection Control Protocols
  • Neurological Monitoring

The SICU provides intensive post-surgical care, ensuring optimal recovery and management of complex surgical cases.

Medical Facilities

Modern Operation Theatres
  • State-of-the-art surgical equipment
  • Sterilized and safe environment
  • Advanced imaging integration

Our operation theatres are equipped with the latest surgical technology to perform a wide range of complex procedures.

Well-Equipped Labor Rooms
  • Comfortable and supportive environment
  • Experienced obstetrics team
  • Fetal monitoring systems

Providing expectant mothers with a safe and caring environment for childbirth, supported by our expert team.

24/7 In-house Pharmacy
  • Wide range of medications
  • Available round-the-clock
  • Timely refills and consultation

Our fully stocked pharmacy ensures that patients have access to all necessary medications at any time of day or night.

Other Essential Facilities

Cafeteria & Dietary Services
  • Healthy and hygienic meals
  • Variety of food options
  • Special dietary requirements

A clean and comfortable cafeteria offering nutritious meals for patients, visitors, and staff.

24/7 Emergency Transport

  • Rapid response system
