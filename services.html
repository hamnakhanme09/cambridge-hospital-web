Comprehensive Hospital Services

A complete spectrum of healthcare services, delivered with expertise and compassion, to meet every patient's needs.

Our Core Patient Services

Accident and Emergency (A&E)

Our **24/7 Emergency Department** is staffed by highly trained emergency physicians, nurses, and support staff, ready to handle all critical and urgent medical conditions. We operate a fully-equipped resuscitation area, trauma bays, and fast-track zones for minor injuries. We are dedicated to providing immediate, life-saving care.

  • **Trauma Center:** Equipped for major accidents and injuries.
  • **24-Hour Critical Care:** Immediate access to specialized medical teams.
  • **Rapid Assessment:** Triage system for quick prioritization of cases.

Certified Blood Bank

Our state-of-the-art **Blood Bank** ensures a safe, adequate, and reliable supply of blood and blood components for transfusions. We strictly adhere to international standards for screening, storage, and processing. We encourage community members to donate to save lives.

  • **Component Separation:** Providing Packed Red Cells, Platelets, and Fresh Frozen Plasma.
  • **Safe Donation:** Modern and sterile donation facilities.
  • **24/7 Availability:** Supporting all hospital departments, especially surgery and A&E.

Outpatient Clinics

We offer comprehensive **Outpatient Clinic** services across all major medical and surgical specialties. Our clinics provide scheduled appointments for diagnostics, consultations, follow-up care, and minor procedures, focusing on preventative care and chronic disease management.

  • General Medicine Clinic
  • Pediatrics & Child Health
  • Obstetrics and Gynecology
  • Dermatology & Skin Care
  • Orthopaedics & Physiotherapy
  • ENT & Eye Clinics
  • Diabetics & Endocrine Clinic
  • Cardiology & Chest Clinic
  • Gastroenterology Clinic

Day Care Services

Our dedicated **Day Care Unit** is designed for patients who require minor procedures, chemotherapy, diagnostic tests, or short-term medical infusions without the need for an overnight stay. This service offers a convenient, comfortable, and cost-effective alternative to full inpatient admission.

Key Features:

  • Post-surgical recovery for minor operations.
  • Infusion and injection administration for chronic conditions.
  • Pain management procedures.

Advanced Operation Theaters

Cambridge Hospital operates multiple, fully-integrated **Operation Theaters (OTs)** equipped with cutting-edge surgical technology and monitored by expert anesthesiologists and surgical teams. We accommodate a wide range of procedures from routine surgeries to complex, minimally invasive, and robotic-assisted operations.

Surgical Capabilities:

  • Laparoscopic and Endoscopic Surgery (Minimally Invasive)
  • Cardiothoracic and Neuro-Surgery suites.
  • Advanced Infection Control protocols.

Professional Nursing Services

The backbone of patient care, our **Nursing Services** provide 24-hour compassionate and clinical support. Our nurses are highly trained in critical care, patient education, medication administration, and psychological support, ensuring a holistic recovery environment for all inpatients.

Key Areas:

  • Inpatient Ward Nursing (Medical/Surgical)
  • Intensive Care Unit (ICU) and Coronary Care Unit (CCU) specialists.
  • Patient and family education on post-discharge care.

Comprehensive Pharmacy Services

Our **Inpatient and Outpatient Pharmacy** provides accurate, timely, and safe medication dispensing. Staffed by registered pharmacists, we offer medication counseling, drug interaction screening, and ensure the availability of all necessary and specialized pharmaceuticals.

Features:

  • 24/7 Inpatient Drug Dispensing.
  • Clinical Pharmacist consultation for complex regimens.
  • Adherence to strict quality control and storage standards.

Patient Social Services

Our **Medical Social Workers** are here to assist patients and families with the challenges of illness and recovery. We provide emotional support, financial assistance guidance, discharge planning, and connections to community resources to ensure a smooth transition back home.

Support Includes:

  • Crisis intervention and counseling.
  • Financial aid and eligibility assistance.
  • Post-discharge care coordination.

Oncology Support Services

Beyond clinical treatment, our **Oncology Support Services** focus on the holistic well-being of cancer patients. This interdisciplinary approach addresses the physical, psychological, social, and spiritual needs of those undergoing cancer care, from diagnosis through survivorship.

Dedicated Programs:

  • Nutritional Counseling and Rehabilitation.
  • Pain and Palliative Care.
  • Oncology-specific psychological support groups.
