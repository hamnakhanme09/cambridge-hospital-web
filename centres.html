Centres of Excellence

Focused, specialized care delivered by leading experts in state-of-the-art facilities. Discover our dedicated centers.

Specialized Care for Complex Needs

Cancer Care Centre

Advanced Oncology & Compassionate Support

The **Cancer Care Centre** offers comprehensive, multidisciplinary oncology services, combining the latest technology with a deeply human approach. Our team of oncologists, surgeons, radiologists, and support staff work together to provide personalized treatment plans, including chemotherapy, radiation therapy, and targeted therapies.

Our Specialized Services:

  • Cutting-edge Linac-based Radiation Therapy
  • Surgical Oncology (minimally invasive techniques)
  • Medical Oncology and Chemotherapy suites
  • Palliative and Pain Management Services
  • Nutritional and Psychological Support

Heart & Vascular Institute

Cardiology, Cardiac Surgery, and Prevention

The **Heart & Vascular Institute** is dedicated to the diagnosis, treatment, and prevention of cardiovascular diseases. We utilize advanced diagnostic imaging, state-of-the-art cath labs, and a skilled team of cardiothoracic surgeons and interventional cardiologists to perform complex procedures with precision and care.

Key Areas of Expertise:

  • 24/7 Emergency Cardiac Care and Interventions
  • Coronary Artery Bypass Grafting (CABG)
  • Electrophysiology and Pacemaker Implantation
  • Non-invasive Cardiac Imaging (Echocardiography, CT Angio)
  • Cardiac Rehabilitation Programs

Neuroscience Centre

Advanced Neurological and Neurosurgical Treatments

Our **Neuroscience Centre** provides specialized care for disorders of the brain, spine, and nervous system. We house some of the region's top neurologists and neurosurgeons, equipped with advanced imaging and surgical navigation systems for procedures like complex spinal surgery and tumor removal.

Advanced Treatments Offered:

  • Stroke and Epilepsy Management Programs
  • Microsurgery for Brain and Spinal Tumors
  • Deep Brain Stimulation (DBS) for movement disorders
  • Comprehensive Neuro-Rehabilitation
  • Advanced Diagnostic EEG and EMG Services

Maternal & Child Health

Comprehensive Care for Women and Children

Our **Maternal & Child Health Centre** provides a supportive and safe environment for every stage of motherhood and childhood. From personalized prenatal care and advanced labor and delivery suites to specialized pediatric medicine, we are dedicated to the well-being of mothers and their children.

Services Include:

  • High-risk Obstetrics and Neonatal Intensive Care Unit (NICU)
  • Painless Delivery options and modern Birthing Suites
  • Specialized Pediatric Subspecialties (e.g., Cardiology, Neurology)
  • Family Planning and Women's Health Clinics

Orthopaedic Centre

Musculoskeletal Health and Sports Medicine

The **Orthopaedic Centre** provides comprehensive care for conditions affecting bones, joints, and muscles. Our experts specialize in joint replacement surgery, sports injury management, trauma, and spine surgery, utilizing minimally invasive techniques for faster recovery times.

Core Specialties:

  • Total Hip and Knee Replacement Surgery
  • Arthroscopic and Sports Injury Repair (ACL, Meniscus)
  • Spine Surgery and Pain Management
  • Pediatric Orthopaedics
  • Fracture and Trauma Management

Rehabilitation Services

Restoring Function and Quality of Life

Our **Rehabilitation Services** are designed to help patients regain strength, mobility, and independence after injury, surgery, or illness (like stroke). Our team of physical, occupational, and speech therapists create intensive, goal-oriented programs tailored to each patient's specific needs and recovery goals.

Therapy Programs:

  • Post-Surgical and Orthopaedic Physical Therapy
  • Neurological Rehabilitation (Stroke, Spinal Cord Injury)
  • Occupational Therapy for daily living skills
  • Speech and Language Pathology

Wellness & Preventive Care

Holistic Health and Long-term Well-being

More than just treating illness, our **Wellness & Preventive Care Centre** focuses on proactive health management. We offer bespoke health screening packages, lifestyle counseling, chronic disease risk assessment, and personalized vaccination schedules to help you maintain optimal health.

Preventative Services Include:

  • Executive Health Checkups and Screenings
  • Weight Management and Nutrition Counseling
  • Smoking Cessation Programs
  • Stress Management and Mental Wellness Support
  • Age-specific Immunization Programs

Academics

At Cambridge Hospital, we are committed not only to healing but also to learning and innovation. Our strong Academic Program trains the next generation of medical leaders through rigorous residency and fellowship programs.
